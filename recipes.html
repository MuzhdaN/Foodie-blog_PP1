Italian Piiza

Ingredients

  1. 2 tablespoons unsalted butter or extra-virgin olive oil*
  2. ¼ cup mayonnaise
  3. 3 ½ cups corn kernels (16 ounces frozen or from about 5 fresh cobs)
  4. ½ teaspoon fine salt
  5. 2 medium cloves garlic, pressed or minced
