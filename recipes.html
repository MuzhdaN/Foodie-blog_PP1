Ramen

Ingredients

  1. 2 (3 oz) packs dried ramen noodles optional: fresh jalapeño or chili slices, for serving
  2. 2 chicken breasts (boneless, skin-on)
  3. kosher salt and freshly-ground black pepper, to season
  4. 1 Tbsp unsalted butter and 2 tsp sesame or vegetable oil
  5. 1 oz dried shitake mushrooms (or 1/2 cup fresh)

instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375℉. Season the chicken generously with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat the oil in a large pot over medium heat until shimmering.
  3. Add the soy sauce and mirin, and stir to combine. Cook for another minute.
  4. Meanwhile, chop the scallions and jalapeño (if using). Slice the chicken into thin pieces.Set aside.
  5. Fill a pot with enough water to cover the eggs, and bring to a boil.

Hawaiian Poke

Ingredients

  1. Poke
  2. Seasoned white rice!
  3. arious other toppings of your choice: pickled ginger, cucumber salad, avocado, mango, furikake, seaweed salad are all excellent add-ons!
  4. Mayonnaise and Wasabi paste
  5. Lemon juice and Sesame oil

instructions

  1. Make white rice: Rinse the rice and simmer it, covered, for 18 minutes
  2. Make the salmon and ahi poke! Gently stir together the cubed, raw ahi tuna and salmon with soy sauce.
  3. Make the cucumber salad. Stir sliced cucumber with a simple dressing of rice vinegar, sugar, salt, red pepper flakes, and sesame oil.
  4. Make the wasabi citrus sauce!
  5. divide the rice between 4 bowls, top with poke, cucumber salad and any of the desired toppings.
